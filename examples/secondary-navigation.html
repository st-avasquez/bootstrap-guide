Breadcrumbs

Breadcrumbs shows the path taken to arrive at the current page or content. They are useful in hierarchical websites, acting as a guide to users on how they arrived at the current page and allowing easy traversal through your website structure.

Pagination

Paginations indicate a series of related content spread across multiple pages. They provide a way for users to quickly view ordered content.
